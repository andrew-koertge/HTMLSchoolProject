Castlevania

Want a harder experience? After beating the game on normal mode, start a new game but without turning off the console. The game will play again, but this time it will be harder.

Excitebike

Need to get back on your bike quickly after crashing? By holding B while recovering, your character will run faster.

Final Fantasy

Here's a pointless but fun easter egg. By hitting A+B exactly 55 times, you'll be taken to a sliding puzzle mini-game. There is no reward for completing it but it's a neat addition.
