Our Story

Started in 1987, the Nintendo Fun Club was a free club offered by Nintendo. It was free to join and members received a special magazine that discussed popular games and games that were planned for the near future. It offered tips, tricks, Nintendo video game news, and comics. With the cancellation of Nintendo Power, now is the time for a revival of a truly legendary service made for fans of Nintendo young and old, whether you prefer classic gaming or the latest trends.
